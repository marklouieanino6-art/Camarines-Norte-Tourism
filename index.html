Welcome to Camarines Norte

Camarines Norte is a province in the Bicol Region of the Philippines known for its stunning beaches, rich culture, and warm people. It is often called the "Land of Gold" because of its gold-mining history in Paracale.

Overview of Camarines Norte

Why Visit Camarines Norte?

  • Beautiful white sand beaches like Bagasbas and Calaguas
  • Rich Bicolano culture and food
  • Historical sites connected to the Spanish colonial period
  • Friendly locals and affordable travel
  • Surfing and water sports destinations

About the Province

Camarines Norte is located at the northern tip of the Bicol Peninsula. It is bounded by the Philippine Sea to the east, Camarines Sur to the south, and Quezon Province to the west. The province has a total land area of about 2,099 square kilometers.

The capital of the province is Daet, which is also the center of commerce and government. The province is composed of 12 municipalities and 1 component city.

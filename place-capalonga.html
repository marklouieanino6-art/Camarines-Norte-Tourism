Capalonga

About Capalonga

Capalonga is a municipality located in the northern part of Camarines Norte. It is surrounded by mountains, rivers, and coastal areas, making it a great destination for nature lovers and eco-tourists.

The town is known for its mangrove forests, which are important for the local marine ecosystem. There are also small falls and rivers that visitors can explore.

Attractions

  • Mangrove forest areas along the coast
  • Small rivers and fishing areas
  • Scenic mountain views
  • Local fishing village communities

How to Get There

Capalonga is accessible from Daet by bus or jeepney. The travel time is around 1.5 to 2 hours. There are also tricycles available within the municipality to go to specific attractions.

Note: Capalonga is a quiet town, so it is best to bring your own supplies and food as there are limited restaurants in the area.

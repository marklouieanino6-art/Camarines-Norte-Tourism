Bagasbas Beach

About Bagasbas Beach

Bagasbas Beach is located in Daet, the capital of Camarines Norte. It is a well-known surfing destination in the Bicol Region. The beach stretches for several kilometers and is easily accessible from the town proper.

Unlike most Philippine beaches, Bagasbas has dark or grayish sand, which is common in beaches near volcanic areas. Despite this, the beach is still very popular for water sports and relaxation.

Activities

  • Surfing - waves are ideal for beginners and intermediate surfers
  • Swimming
  • Beach volleyball
  • Photography and sunset viewing
  • Renting surfboards from local shops

How to Get There

From Daet town center, you can ride a tricycle or jeepney going to Bagasbas Beach. The travel time is about 10 to 15 minutes. There are also resorts and food stalls near the beach area.

Tip: The best surfing months at Bagasbas are from October to March when the waves are stronger. If you are a beginner, surf lessons are available from local instructors at a low cost.

Matabungkay Coastal Area

About Matabungkay

Matabungkay is a coastal barangay known for its calm waters and small beach resorts. It is a quieter alternative to the more popular Bagasbas Beach, making it a great choice for families and those looking for a relaxing stay.

The area features small cottages for rent along the shoreline and several local food stalls selling fresh seafood dishes.

Things to Do

  • Swimming in the calm coastal waters
  • Renting beachside cottages for day trips
  • Eating fresh seafood at nearby eateries
  • Watching the sunrise or sunset by the shore
Tip: Matabungkay is a good budget-friendly option. Cottage rentals are usually affordable and the seafood is fresh and cheap.

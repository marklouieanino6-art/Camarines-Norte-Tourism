Bagasbas Beach

Overview

Bagasbas Beach is the most popular beach in Camarines Norte. Located in Daet, it is known as one of the top surfing destinations in the Bicol Region. The beach stretches for about 2 kilometers and faces the Pacific Ocean, which gives it strong and consistent waves.

Surfing at Bagasbas

Surfing is the main activity at Bagasbas. The waves are suitable for beginners, intermediate, and sometimes advanced surfers depending on the season. Surfboards can be rented from local shops near the beach, and surf instructors are available for those who want to learn.

  • Best surfing months: October to March
  • Wave height: 1 to 3 meters depending on the season
  • Surfboard rental: available from local vendors
  • Surf lessons: available from local instructors

Other Activities

  • Swimming (caution needed during strong waves)
  • Beach walks and jogging along the shoreline
  • Photography and watching local surfers
  • Eating at beachside restaurants and food stalls

Getting There

From Daet town proper, take a tricycle or jeepney going to Bagasbas. Travel time is about 10 to 15 minutes. The fare is very affordable. You can also walk from the town center if you enjoy a short stroll.

Safety Tip: Always check the wave conditions before swimming. The beach can have strong currents during typhoon season (June to November). Follow the advice of local lifeguards.

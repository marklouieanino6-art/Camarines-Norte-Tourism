Festivals of Camarines Norte

Experience the vibrant culture and traditions of Camarines Norte through its colorful festivals and celebrations held throughout the year.

Local Festivals and Events

Siram Festival - Daet

The Siram Festival is celebrated every May in Daet, the capital of Camarines Norte. The word "siram" means "to pour water" in Bikol. It is a thanksgiving festival for the arrival of the rainy season after a long summer. Locals celebrate by splashing water on each other in the streets, similar to water festivals in other parts of Asia.

The festival also includes street dancing, parades, cultural shows, and food fairs. It is one of the most lively and fun festivals in the province.

Paracale Gold Festival

The Paracale Gold Festival is held every year to celebrate the gold mining heritage of Paracale. The festival features cultural performances, exhibits of locally made gold jewelry, street parades, and beauty pageants. It is a proud celebration of the town's identity as the Gold Capital of the Philippines.

Fiesta Celebrations

Each municipality in Camarines Norte celebrates its own town fiesta in honor of its patron saint. These fiestas are an important part of Filipino culture and usually include:

  • Religious processions and church masses
  • Street dancing and cultural performances
  • Local food and delicacies
  • Sports events and games
  • Beauty pageants
